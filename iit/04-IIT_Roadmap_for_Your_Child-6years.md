# A **6-year IIT preparation roadmap** from **Class 6 → 12**, keeping it **stress-free, balanced, and realistic**.

## I’ll break it **year by year**, including **topics, study hours, books, activities, and parent guidance**.

---

# **6-Year IIT Roadmap for Your Child (Class 6 → 12)**

---

## **Phase 1: Foundation Stage (Class 6–8)**

**Goal:** Build **conceptual understanding, curiosity, and problem-solving skills**.

| Class | Focus Subjects                                                                                                                    | Study Hours (Daily) | Books/Resources                                   | Activities / Parent Role                                                                  |
| ----- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------- | ------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **6** | Math: Fractions, Decimals, Percentages, Geometry basics. Science: NCERT (Physics, Chemistry, Biology). Logic: Puzzles, reasoning. | 1–2 hrs             | NCERT 6, R.S. Aggarwal (Math), SOF Olympiad books | Fun experiments, games, puzzles. Encourage curiosity. Don’t focus on marks.               |
| **7** | Math: Integers, Ratio, Simple Algebra, Perimeter & Area. Science: Light, Sound, Simple Machines.                                  | 1–2 hrs             | NCERT 7, R.S. Aggarwal, Olympiad workbooks        | Science kits, small projects. Encourage questions. Introduce mental math.                 |
| **8** | Math: Algebra basics, Geometry (angles, triangles, circles). Science: Electricity, Magnetism, Chemical reactions.                 | 1–2 hrs             | NCERT 8, Olympiad workbooks                       | Participate in science/Math competitions (optional). Parent: encourage effort, not marks. |

**Parent Tips for Phase 1:**

* Celebrate curiosity & problem-solving
* 20–30 min reading daily (science magazines, stories with logic)
* Introduce light Olympiads for confidence

---

## **Phase 2: Foundation IIT Stage (Class 9–10)**

**Goal:** Build **strong foundation for JEE**, develop problem-solving mindset.

| Class  | Focus Subjects                                                                                                                     | Study Hours (Daily) | Books/Resources                                     | Activities / Parent Role                                                     |
| ------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------- | --------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **9**  | Math: Algebra, Trigonometry, Coordinate Geometry basics. Physics: Motion, Work, Energy. Chemistry: Mole concept, Atomic structure. | 2–3 hrs             | NCERT 9, RD Sharma (Math), HC Verma Vol 1 (Physics) | Weekly small tests, encourage questions. Light Olympiad prep.                |
| **10** | Math: Quadratic eq, Probability, Geometry advanced. Physics: Electricity, Light. Chemistry: Periodic table, Reactions.             | 2–3 hrs             | NCERT 10, Olympiad books                            | Encourage self-study. Focus on **concept mastery**. Mock tests for practice. |

**Parent Tips for Phase 2:**

* Track daily practice, not marks
* Encourage participation in competitions (Math/Science Olympiads)
* Balance studies and hobbies

---

## **Phase 3: Core IIT Prep (Class 11)**

**Goal:** Start **JEE-level preparation** along with school syllabus.

| Subject       | Focus Topics                                                                   | Books/Resources                      |
| ------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------ | ------------------------------------ |
| **Math**      | Algebra, Trigonometry, Calculus (Limits, Differentiation), Coordinate Geometry | NCERT 11, RD Sharma, Cengage for JEE |
| **Physics**   | Mechanics, Laws of Motion, Work, Energy, Gravitation                           | HC Verma Vol 1 & 2                   |
| **Chemistry** | Physical (Thermodynamics, Kinetics), Organic basics, Inorganic                 | NCERT 11, OP Tandon, Arihant         |

**Study Hours:** 4–5 hrs/day (school + coaching)
**Parent Role:**

* Monitor routine, ensure healthy sleep & breaks
* Encourage problem-solving, not rote learning
* Discuss doubts, clarify concepts together

---

## **Phase 4: Core IIT Prep (Class 12)**

**Goal:** Complete **JEE syllabus**, strengthen concepts, start serious problem-solving.

| Subject   | Focus Topics                                      | Books/Resources              |
| --------- | ------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------- |
| Math      | Calculus, Algebra, Vectors, 3D Geometry           | NCERT 12, Cengage, RD Sharma |
| Physics   | Electrostatics, Magnetism, Optics, Modern Physics | HC Verma Vol 2               |
| Chemistry | Physical, Organic, Inorganic (advanced)           | NCERT 12, OP Tandon, Arihant |

**Study Hours:** 5–6 hrs/day (school + coaching)
**Parent Role:**

* Ensure **time management**, regular sleep, and breaks
* Help track mistakes in practice tests
* Keep stress low, celebrate effort

---

## **Phase 5: Final Year Strategy (JEE Mains + Advanced)**

**Goal:** **Speed, accuracy, exam strategy**

* Solve **previous 15–20 years’ JEE papers**
* Take **weekly full-length mock tests**
* Revise formulas, key concepts, problem-solving shortcuts
* Analyze mistakes and weak areas

**Parent Role:**

* Emotional support & stress management
* Ensure child eats well, sleeps enough
* Avoid pressuring them about rank

---

## **Phase 6: Daily Routine for Current Class (6)**

| Time      | Activity                                    |
| --------- | ------------------------------------------- |
| 30–40 min | Math: Concept + 10–15 problems              |
| 30–40 min | Science: Concept + simple experiment        |
| 20 min    | Logic / puzzle / reasoning                  |
| Weekends  | Revision + 1 fun science activity + reading |

---

## **Phase 7: Books & Resources (Class 6–12)**

* **Math:** NCERT, R.S. Aggarwal, RD Sharma, Cengage (11–12)
* **Physics:** NCERT, HC Verma Vol 1 & 2, Olympiad workbooks
* **Chemistry:** NCERT, OP Tandon, Arihant, Olympiad books
* **Olympiad / Reasoning:** SOF, NTSE level material

---

## ✅ **Parent Checklist (Long-term)**

1. Encourage **curiosity over marks** (Class 6–8)
2. Build **self-study habit** (Class 9–10)
3. Track **problem-solving ability** (Class 11–12)
4. Support **healthy lifestyle**: sleep, exercise, hobbies
5. Avoid comparison or pressuring for rank
6. Celebrate **effort milestones**, not just results
